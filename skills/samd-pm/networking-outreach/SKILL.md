# Skill: Networking Outreach

## Purpose
Draft personalized LinkedIn messages or emails for networking outreach. Direct, confident, not sycophantic. References contact context from `knowledge/people/`.

## When to Use
- "Write a LinkedIn message to [person]"
- "Draft an outreach email for [person at company]"
- "Help me reach out to someone at [company]"

## Instructions

### Before Drafting
1. Check if `knowledge/people/[name].md` exists. If yes, read it for context.
2. If no file exists, ask for: who the person is, how they know them (or if cold), and what they want from the outreach.
3. Determine the channel: LinkedIn connection request (300 char limit), LinkedIn message, or email.

### Drafting Rules
- **Keep it short.** LinkedIn connection requests: 2-3 sentences max. Messages/emails: 4-6 sentences.
- **Lead with context, not flattery.** "I saw your team launched X" not "I'm a huge fan of your incredible work."
- **Be specific about the ask.** "Would you have 15 minutes to chat about how your team handles SaMD design controls?" not "I'd love to pick your brain sometime."
- **Show value, don't beg.** Position as a peer with relevant domain expertise, not a supplicant.
- **No buzzwords.** No "synergy," "leverage," "passionate about." Write like a human.

### Templates

**Cold — LinkedIn Connection Request (< 300 chars)**
```
Hi [Name] — I'm a healthcare PM with [X] years in SaMD ([notable companies]). [One specific thing about their work/company]. Would love to connect and [specific reason].
```

**Warm — LinkedIn Message (have a mutual connection or shared context)**
```
Hi [Name] — [Mutual connection] mentioned you / I saw your [specific thing].

I'm a healthcare PM specializing in software medical devices — [brief career summary with relevant companies].

I'm exploring [specific area they work in] and would value your perspective on [specific question]. Would you have 15 minutes this week or next?
```

**Email — Informational Interview Request**
```
Subject: Quick question on [specific topic] from a fellow healthcare PM

Hi [Name],

I'm [PM name] — healthcare PM with [X] years building FDA-cleared software medical devices, most recently at [company].

I'm [exploring roles in X / building a project in Y / researching Z] and [their company/team] caught my attention because [specific reason].

Would you have 15 minutes for a quick call? I'm particularly curious about [one specific question].

Happy to work around your schedule. Thanks for considering it.

[PM name]
```

### After Sending
- Update `knowledge/people/[name].md` with the touchpoint
- Set a follow-up reminder (1 week if no response)

### Follow-Up (No Response After 1 Week)
```
Hi [Name] — following up on my note from last week. I know you're busy — happy to do a quick 10-minute call whenever works. If now isn't a good time, no worries at all.
```

**One follow-up only.** If no response after the follow-up, move on.
