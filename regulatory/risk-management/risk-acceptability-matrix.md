# Risk Acceptability Matrix

> Defined per the Risk Management Plan. Referenced by the risk-management skill and all risk analysis artifacts.
> Per ISO 14971:2019 Clause 4.4 — risk acceptability criteria shall be defined in the Risk Management Plan.

## 5×5 Severity × Probability Matrix

```
         P1    P2    P3    P4    P5
S5       A     U     U     U     U
S4       A     A     U     U     U
S3       AC    A     A     U     U
S2       AC    AC    A     A     U
S1       AC    AC    AC    AC    A
```

- **AC** = Acceptable (no further action required)
- **A (AFAP)** = Acceptable only with documented justification that risk has been reduced as far as possible (ISO 14971:2019)
- **U** = Unacceptable (must implement risk controls before release)

## Customization
Teams should review and adjust this matrix based on their Risk Management Plan. Changes to risk acceptability criteria require change control and regulatory review.
